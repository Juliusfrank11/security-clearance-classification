In Applicant’s answer to the SOR, he admitted all the allegations. His admissions are incorporated in my findings of fact. Applicant  is  a  43-year-old  technician  employed  by  a  defense  contractor  since October 2016. He was employed as an enrollment advisor at a university from April 2015 to October 2016, and he left this job voluntarily to accept his current employment.  He also has been self-employed since August 2005 as a process server. He served on active duty in the U.S. Navy from February 1996 to April 2005 and received an honorable discharge. His security clearance application reflects that he has never held a security clearance.  Applicant married in July 1996 and divorced in May 2005. He has three children, ages 25, 22, and 9. He received a bachelor’s degree in May 2011. The  SOR  alleges  seven  delinquent  debts  totaling  about  $29,852,  which  are reflected  in  credit  reports  from  May  30,  2019;  April  19,  2019;  and  February  10,  2018 .
