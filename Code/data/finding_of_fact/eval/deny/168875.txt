In Applicant’s SOR response, he admitted the SOR allegation in ¶ 1.a.  His admission is accepted as a finding of fact. Additional findings follow. Applicant is a 51-year-old mechanic who supports the Navy.  In 1989, he graduated from high school, and he has not attended college.  In 1993, he joined the Navy, and in 2013, he honorably retired from the Navy as a petty officer second class .  His Navy specialty was mechanic and painter.  In 1993, he married, and in 2000, he divorced.  He married his current spouse in 2008.  His two children are ages 24 and 27.  His spouse is disabled and does not work outside their home.  She receives $1,000 monthly from Social Security disability.  Applicant  was  receiving  50  percent  disability  from  the  Department  of  Veterans Affairs  for migraine headaches and 10 percent disability for his elbow.  He  recently  applied  for  additional  disability  for  post-traumatic  stress,  and  the  VA eliminated  his  disability  for  migraine  headaches.    He  is  appealing  the  VA’s assessments of  his disabilities.
