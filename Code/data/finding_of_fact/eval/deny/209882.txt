After  a  thorough  and  careful  review  of  the  pleadings,  exhibits,  and testimony, I make the following findings of fact. Applicant  is  a  69-year-old  employee  of  a  defense  contractor.  He  has  been employed  with  the  defense  contractor  since  July  of  2020.  Applicant  is  separated  from his spouse and has no children. He is retired from the U.S. Army.   Applicant  attributes  his admitted financial difficulties to a failed business. Guideline F - Financial Considerations 2.a.
