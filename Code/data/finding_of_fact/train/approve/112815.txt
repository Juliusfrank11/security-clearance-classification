Iraq I take administrative notice of the following facts: In 2003, The United States led a  coalition  to  remove  Saddam  Hussein  from  power  in  Iraq.  After  free  elections,  Iraq’s new  government  took  office.  Despite  the  elections  and  new  government,  Iraq  remains engulfed  in  violence,  perpetrated  by  Al  Qaeda  terrorists  and  other  insurgents. Numerous attacks and kidnappings have targeted the U.S. Armed Forces, contractors, and other civilians, as well as Iraqis. Even with aggressive governmental action against terrorists,  the  threat  of  terrorism  in  Iraq  remains  high.
