Applicant is a 49-year-old high school graduate who retired from the U.S. military in late  August  2016  after  over  24  years  of  active-duty  service.  He  served  honorably  and achieved  the  rank  of  chief  petty  officer.  He  has  worked  for  a  defense  contractor  as  a uniformed protection officer since August 2016.  Applicant was granted a secret clearance in July 2014.  His employer has requested that he obtain a top secret clearance.  Applicant and his spouse have been married since March 1994, although they have been together longer.  They have two daughters, ages 24 and 27. Applicant adopted his older daughter when she was three years old.  2 Applicant directly supported Operation Enduring Freedom, and he was awarded several decorations and medals for meritorious military service.   Because of his various military deployments, including 21 months in Southwest Asia,1 his spouse handled their household finances with little oversight from him.  Applicant’s spouse worked as a laboratory technician part time, earning $30,000 a year before July 2007.  In 2008, she was diagnosed with cancer.
