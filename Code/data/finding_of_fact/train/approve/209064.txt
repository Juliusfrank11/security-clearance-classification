Applicant  denied  the  allegations  in  SOR  ¶¶  1.a,  2.a.  and  3.  a.  After  a  thorough and  careful  review  of  the  pleadings,  exhibits,  and  testimony,  I  make  the  following findings of fact. Applicant  is  a  26-year-old  employee  of  a  defense  contractor.  He  has  been employed with the defense contractor since April of 2020. Applicant is married, and has no  children.   Guideline   J   -  Criminal  Conduct,  Guideline   D   -  Sexual   Behavior,   &  Guideline   E   - Personal Conduct 1.a., 2.a. and 3.a. In about October of 2017, more than five years ago  while on active duty with the Marine Corps, Applicant attempted to record and take photos of a female sailor, while she was changing her clothes in a changing room. This attempt was done  without  her  knowledge  or  consent.   As a result, Applicant was convicted at  a  Summary  Court-Martial    for  Attempt  to  record  and  take  pictures  of  a  Naval member of the Armed Forces without consent.  As a result of this conviction, Applicant was  reduced  in  rank  from  a  Lance  Corporal    to  a  Private  First  Class  ,  and discharged in about  December of 2019 with an Other Than Honorable Discharge.   Applicant’s  wife,  who  was  his girlfriend in 2017, is aware of Applicant’s criminal conduct.
