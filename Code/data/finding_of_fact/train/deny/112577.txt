Applicant is 50 years old, divorced, but remarried.  He works for a defense contractor, but does not currently have a security clearance.  Guideline H – Drug Involvement 1.a. Applicant began using marijuana, at the age of 18, in about 1987; and used the illegal substance for about ten years, “mostly on weekends,” until about 1996.  He resumed his marijuana use in about 2010 “when . . . [his] divorce was in full swing and . . . [Applicant] felt sad . . . [and] alone.”  He used  marijuana,  “two  or  three  times  a  week,”  until  his  last  usage  in  June  of  2017,  to treat  his  “anxiety.”   1.b.  During  a  June  2017  interview  with  an  investigator  for  DOD,  Applicant averred  that  he  would  continue  to  use  marijuana  until  his  self-diagnosed  anxiety,  and related  stress,  subsided.
