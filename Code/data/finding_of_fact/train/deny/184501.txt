Previously,  he  worked  for  other  employers  in  various 2 research positions. He reported intermittent periods of  unemployment between  August 2011 and March 2018.  Applicant’s drug history Throughout his college years , Applicant used and purchased illegal drugs  for his personal use.  Records document  that he purchased and used marijuana with varying frequency, from about May 2011 to at least July 2017; purchased and used LSD with  varying  frequency from  2015  to  about  2016;  purchased and  used  Psilocybin Mushrooms  and  other  hallucinogens    with  varying  frequency,  from about  August  2014  to  about  2017;  purchased  and  used  cocaine  on  at  least  one occasion in July 2014, and purchased and used MDA on at least one occasion in July 2014.  In  a  personal subject  interview   conducted  by  an  investigator from  the  Office of   Personnel  Management     in  October  2018,  Applicant  acknowledged   frequent smoking  of  marijuana  in college  between  2014  and  2017. Based   on   his  OPM   interview   responses,   no   clear and reliable  inferences   can   be   drawn   about   Applicant’s  risks  of  recurrent  involvement   with illegal drugs.
