Applicant  admitted  to  the  allegations  in  SOR  ¶¶  1.c.  through  1.g.  She  denied
SOR allegations ¶¶ 1.a. and 1.b. After a thorough and careful review of the pleadings,
exhibits, and testimony, I make the following findings of fact.
Applicant  is  a  32-year-old  employee  of  a  defense  contractor.  (GX  1  at  pages  7
and  15.)  She  has  been  employed  with  the  defense  contractor  since  October  of  2017.
Applicant  held  a  security  clearance  during  her  nine  years  of  active  duty  with  the  U.S.
Navy from 2007~2016. (GX 1 at pages 21 and 34, and TR at page 12 line 25 to page 15
line 15.) She is divorced, and has one child. (TR at page 15 lines 4~11.)
Guideline F - Financial Considerations
1.a.  and  1.b.  Applicant  denies  these  allegations,  but  admits  that  she  co-signed
with her mother for two past-due debts owed to Creditor A totaling about $23,616. (TR
at  page  15  line  19  to  page  18  line  11,  and  GX  2  at  page  3.)  Applicant  avers  that  her
mother is pursuing the discharge of these debts through a bankruptcy, but has offered
nothing  further  in  this  regard.  (TR  at  page  15  line  19  to  page  18  line  11.)  These
allegations are found against Applicant.
1.c. Applicant admits that she is indebted to Creditor C for $6,435 as the result of
automobile  repairs.  (TR  at  page  18  line  12  to  page  22  line  11.)  Despite  having  been
given  a  month  to  do  so,  Applicant  has  submitted  nothing  further  in  this  regard.  This
allegation is found against Applicant.
1.d.  Applicant admits that she is indebted to Creditor D for about $3,071. (TR at
page  22  line  12  to  page  22  line  23.)  Despite  having  been  given  a  month  to  do  so,
Applicant  has  submitted  nothing  further  in  this  regard.  This allegation  is found  against
Applicant.
1.e.  Applicant  admits  that  she  is  indebted  to  Creditor  E  for  about  $330.  (TR  at
page  24  line  22  to  page  26  line  1.)  Despite  having  been  given  a  month  to  do  so,
Applicant  has  submitted  nothing  further  in  this  regard.  This allegation  is found  against
Applicant.
1.f. and 1.g.  Applicant admits that she is indebted to Creditor F for two past-due
debts totaling about $19,409. (TR at page 26 line 2 to page 28 line 23.) Despite having
been  given  a  month  to  do  so,  Applicant  has  submitted  nothing  further  in  this  regard.
These allegations are found against Applicant.
