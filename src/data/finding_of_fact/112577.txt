Guideline H – Drug Involvement

the illegal substance for about ten years, “mostly on weekends,” until about 1996. (TR at
page 18 line 17 to page 29 line 4.) He resumed his marijuana use in about 2010 “when .

used  marijuana,  “two  or  three  times  a  week,”  until  his  last  usage  in  June  of  2017,  to
treat  his  “anxiety.”  (TR  at  page  18  line  17  to  page  29  line  4,  and  at  page  36  lines
11~17.)

averred  that  he  would  continue  to  use  marijuana  until  his  self-diagnosed  anxiety,  and
related  stress,  subsided.  (TR  at  page  29  line  9  to  page  31  line  6.)  At  his  hearing,
Applicant recanted this averment. (TR at page 31 line 7 to page 34 line 9.)
