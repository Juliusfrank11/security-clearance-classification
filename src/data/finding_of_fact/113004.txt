Applicant  admitted  to  all  the  allegations  in  SOR.  After  a  thorough  and  careful
review of the pleadings, exhibits, and testimony, I make the following findings of fact.
Applicant  is  a  52-year-old  employee  of  a  defense  contractor.  (GX  1  at  pages  5
and 10.)  She has been employed with the defense contractor since June of 2016. She

months after the issuance of the SOR. (GX 7.) Her debts have not yet been discharged.
