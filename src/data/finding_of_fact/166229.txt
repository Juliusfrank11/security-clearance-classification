After a thorough and careful review of the pleadings and exhibits submitted, I make
the following findings of fact.
Applicant is 32 years old, was divorced in November 2019, and has no children.
He  is  a  high  school  graduate  and  served  in  the  U.S.  Air  Force  from  2008  through
November 2017, when he was honorably discharged.  (Item 3.)
The SOR alleged that Applicant has three delinquent debts totaling over just over
$32,000.  (Item  1.)  Applicant  admitted  those  allegations.  He  claimed  that  his
delinquencies were caused by being unemployed from November 2017 until September
2018 when he was hired by his current employer.  (Item 2.) The record shows no other
periods  of  unemployment  for  Applicant.  (Item  3.)  In  his  August  2,  2021  Response,
Applicant submitted documents evidencing that in July 2021 he successfully resolved his
SOR delinquencies.
When  the  FORM  was  submitted,  the  SOR  debts  were  delinquent.  (Item  4.)
Applicant had not previousy submitted any documents showing that he was addressing
those debts.
