Financial Considerations

receiving his mail. (Tr. 27) In 2018, he moved to a different state, and he lived with his
sister. (Tr. 27) From 2018 to 2020, his annual salary was about $25,000. (Tr. 22) In 2020,

(Tr. 27; AE A)
